About Him

Muhammad Ali, originally Cassius Clay, was one of the greatest boxers and cultural icons in history. Known as "The Greatest," he revolutionized boxing with his speed, skill, and charisma. Ali became the World Heavyweight Champion three times and won a gold medal at the 1960 Olympics in Rome.
